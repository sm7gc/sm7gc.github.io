Sanjana Mendu

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Surgery at Harvard Medical School working in the Medical Robotics and Computer Assisted Surgery Lab led by Dr. Marco A. Zenati.

I received my PhD in Informatics at Penn State, where I was advised by Dr. Saeed Abdullah. Before joining Penn State, I graduated from the University of Virginia with a B.S. in Computer Science in 2018 and a M.S. in Systems Engineering in 2020. There, I worked in the Sensing Systems for Health Lab led by Dr. Laura Barnes.
